## Distribution Groups

* Distribution groups are also known as **distribution lists**
* They are **email-only groups**
* A distribution group is represented by an **email address**
* The main purpose of this email address is to **send emails to all members of the group**
* **Important:** Do not use distribution groups for **access control**

**Examples:**

* `s-biology@contoso.edu`
* `s-physics@contoso.edu`
* `staff-academic@contoso.edu`
* `it-support@contoso.edu`
* `lab-chemistry@contoso.edu`, etc
